ABOUT ME

Hey there, glad you’re here!

I’m Isaiah — a passionate front-end developer and graphic designer with a drive for solving problems, building beautiful experiences, and helping small businesses thrive. With a few solid years of industry experience under my belt, I’m constantly learning, taking on new challenges, and collaborating with amazing teams to bring ideas to life.

When I’m not designing or coding, you’ll find me outside doing what I love most — swimming, kayaking, running, or hiking through elevated terrain. I’m big on staying active and pushing my physical limits. Indoors, I keep that momentum going with roller skating and lifting at the gym. And when it’s time to slow down and recharge, I dive into one of my favorite hobbies: building LEGO sets and scale models.

Beyond the screen and trail, I’ve always been fascinated by technology and space exploration. The idea of traveling beyond the stars and building sustainable life among the planets is a vision that constantly inspires me. I’m passionate about progress — not just in tech, but in helping people build better lives, stronger communities, and a future worth looking forward to.

Thanks for stopping by — I’m excited to connect and see what we can build together.
